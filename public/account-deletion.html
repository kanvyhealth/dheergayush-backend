Delete Account

Users of the Dheergayush application can request permanent deletion of their account and associated personal information.

Account deletion may remove

  • User profile information
  • Consultation history
  • Uploaded documents
  • Application preferences
  • Authentication records

Certain information may be retained where required for

  • Legal obligations
  • Fraud prevention
  • Security purposes
  • Financial transaction compliance

Deletion Methods

How to Delete Your Account

Option 1:
Inside the mobile application:
Profile → Settings → Delete Account

Option 2:
Send an account deletion request to:
shaikmasthanjavidvali@gmail.com

Please include:

  • Registered mobile number
  • Registered email address
  • Reason for deletion (optional)

Timeline

Account deletion requests are generally processed within 7 business days.
