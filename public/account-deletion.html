Delete Account

You may request permanent deletion of your DHEERGAYUSH account and associated personal data. This action cannot be undone once processed.

What we remove

  • Profile details and authentication records
  • Consultation history and uploaded medical reports
  • Preferences and notification settings

What we may retain

  • Records required for legal, fraud prevention, or financial compliance

Other ways to delete

Mobile app: Profile → Settings → Delete Account

Email: Write to contact@dheergayush.net from your registered email with your mobile number.
