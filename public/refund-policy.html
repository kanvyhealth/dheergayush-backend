Refund Policy

Refund eligibility depends on the consultation outcome and payment type. We aim to process eligible refunds fairly and promptly.

Eligible for refund

  • Video consultation failed to connect or could not start
  • Doctor did not accept within the waiting period
  • Duplicate payments or confirmed platform technical failures

Not typically eligible

  • Successfully completed consultations
  • User-side device or connectivity issues after connection
  • Missed appointments without prior cancellation

Processing time

Approved refunds are initiated to your original payment method within 7–10 business days, depending on your bank or card issuer.
