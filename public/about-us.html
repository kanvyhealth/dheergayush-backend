About Dheergayush

Dheergayush is a healthcare technology platform operated by DHEERGAYUSH INDIA PRIVATE LIMITED.

Our platform is designed to simplify healthcare accessibility by connecting patients with healthcare professionals through secure digital consultations and appointment management services.

We aim to make healthcare services more accessible, reliable, and digitally connected for users across India.

The Dheergayush platform supports

  • Online healthcare consultations
  • Appointment scheduling
  • Digital healthcare communication
  • Prescription and report sharing
  • Notifications and reminders
  • Secure healthcare interaction

Our mission is to improve digital healthcare accessibility while maintaining user privacy, transparency, and service quality.
